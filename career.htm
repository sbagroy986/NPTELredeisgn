Partner with us!
 
Any industry company to partner with us and collaborate in the following ways:-
  • Co- Offer a NPTEL course, the industry person can give lectures along with the Faculty and supply industry/labaratory videos for the specific discipline.
  • Co-Certify a NPTEL course, specialized feild courses can be recognized in that specific Industry. For example , Modern Construction Materials course will be recognized by Cement manufacturing Industry
  • Adopt the courses for internal/fresher training at your Company

Companies can also:
  • get the list of Course Toppers** (on NPTEL) in a particular specialized discipline for Employment offers.
  • post an advertisement inviting applications for jobs/internships with them
For more details, please contact us.
** Only students who have agreed to share their contact information with prospective employers are only considered.
